Choose Multiple Days
  1. Click on the '...' button to display the calendar.
  2. You can now choose multiple days in the calendar.
  3. The following dates have been programmatically chosen
    • 2004 April 25
    • 2004 April 18
    • 2004 April 22
    • 2004 April 05
    You can uncheck these.
  4. When finished click on the 'X' located in the top right hand corner to close the calendar.
  5. The dates are displayed in the text area.
