Double click, with time
  1. Click on the '...' button to display the calendar. Notice that you can choose the time at the bottom of the calendar.
  2. In the calendar select the date by clicking twice on the same date.
  3. The results are inserted in the following format:
    Abbreviated day of the week, Abbreviated month, Day, Year [Hour:Minute AM/PM] (i.e.: Mon, Jan 1, 2007 [03:48 PM])
