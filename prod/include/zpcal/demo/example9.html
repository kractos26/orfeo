Update drop down menus from the calendar
Instead of a text box, the dates are saved in drop down menus.
  1. Click on the '...' button to display the calendar.
  2. Notice, as you change the dates in the calendar, the drop down menus change too
  3. Choose your date, and the drop down menus reflect your choice
  4. The results will display in the following format:
    Year-Month-Day
